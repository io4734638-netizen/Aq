⚔️ **BATTLE ROYALE ARENA** ⚔️

A fast-paced web-based battle royale game where you fight against AI enemies in a shrinking arena!

## Features

🎮 **Gameplay**
- Single-player battle royale with 10 AI enemies
- Shrinking danger zone that forces players into combat
- Health and ammo pickups scattered across the map
- Real-time combat mechanics

🕹️ **Controls**
- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** - Move your character
- **SPACE** - Attack (uses ammo)
- **E** - Pickup nearby items
- **ESC** - Pause (coming soon)

📊 **Game Mechanics**
- **Health System** - Your health depletes in the danger zone; heal by picking up red items
- **Ammo System** - Each attack costs ammo; find yellow items to replenish
- **AI Enemies** - Smart enemies that hunt you, avoid danger zones, and fight each other
- **Shrinking Zone** - The playable area shrinks over time, forcing confrontation

🎯 **Win Conditions**
- **Victory** - Defeat all 10 enemies
- **Defeat** - Your health reaches 0 or you stay in the danger zone too long

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click "START GAME" to begin
3. Move around the arena and defeat enemies
4. Manage your health and ammo by picking up items
5. Avoid the red danger zone
6. Be the last player standing!

## Files

- `index.html` - Game structure and UI
- `style.css` - Styling and animations
- `game.js` - Game logic, physics, and AI

## Tips & Strategy

💡 **Pro Tips**
- Stay in the safe zone (blue circle) as long as possible
- Stock up on ammo before engaging enemies
- Use the terrain to your advantage and corner enemies
- Heal up when you find health packs
- Listen to zone warnings and move early
- Let enemies fight each other while you gather supplies
